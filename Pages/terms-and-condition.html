1. Service Description

HugPups provides a temporary therapy dog companion service designed to support mental health. Our services include:

  • Rent a Dog: A one-week temporary companionship service where a trained dog stays at your residence.
  • Visit the Team: Scheduled office visits to meet our staff and therapy animals.

2. Nature of Service

  • Not a Substitute for Professional Care: HugPups is an addition to therapy, not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
  • Educational Purpose: Our service aims to provide experience with animals to help individuals transition toward potential future pet ownership.

3. User Responsibilities

  • Application Accuracy: Users must provide truthful and accurate information regarding their living situation and mental health needs during the application process.
  • Animal Welfare: Users renting a dog are responsible for providing a safe and caring environment for the animal for the duration of the one-week period.
  • Supervision: During "Visit the Team" sessions, users must follow all staff instructions to ensure the safety of both humans and animals.

4. Booking and Cancellations

All service requests (Rent a Dog and Office Visits) are subject to availability and a successful application review.

HugPups reserves the right to deny an application if the environment is deemed unsuitable for our therapy dogs.
